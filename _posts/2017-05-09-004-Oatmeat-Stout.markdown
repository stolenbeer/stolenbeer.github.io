> **Verdict:** A straightforward (and malt forward), enjoyable oatmeal stout! I will brew this again, both as is or bittered with Northern Brewer or Columbus hops.


## Recipe ##
**Vol:** 10L (Half batch)  
**Targets:**

| OG | FG | ABV | IBU |
|----|----|-----|-----|
| 1.055 | 1.014 | 5.4% | 26 |

### Grist ###

| Step            | time | temp  |
|-----------------|------|-------|
