> **Verdict:** A straightforward (and malt forward), enjoyable oatmeal stout! I will brew this again, perhaps with alternative bittering hops.


## Recipe ##
**Vol:** 10L (Half batch)  
**Mash:**  60 min @ 66°C  
**Yeast:** Safale S04 English Ale   
**Targets:**

{:.table}
| OG | FG | ABV | IBU |
|----|----|-----|-----|
| 1.055 | 1.014 | 5.4% | 26 |

| Malt               | Weight     |        |

| Oats Flaked        | 125 g      | (10%)  |
| Chocolate Malt     | 70  g      | (6%)   |
